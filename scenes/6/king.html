The carvings told the story of the Hurion, a mysterious race that once lived in the land of the lost city. Long ago, when humans discovered them, they exploited the Hurion’s ability to float, forcing them to build vast monuments and suffering under their cruelty. But then, a being unlike any other—a creature resembling a great dinosaur—rose to defend them. Wielding the power of time itself, this guardian transported the entire city back to the age of the dinosaurs. There, the Hurion thrived in peace, and the dinosaur-like being was crowned their king. For a time, they lived in harmony, but tragedy struck when their protector vanished. With his passing, the time magic shattered, pulling the city back into the present. Thus, the city became a tale of history and legend, remembered but never seen… until now
