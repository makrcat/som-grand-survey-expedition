The Cringe Canyon

The Cringe Canyon is a popular site of tourism on the Island.

A core part of the Meme Trail, the Cringe Canyon contains carvings of all memes that have passed through it.

After going through the Cringe Borderlands and the Cringe Peak, memes pass by this canyon on their way to the final stop, the Tomb of Giggles.

Passing by the Cringe Canyon, the memes are sculpted into the canyon and forever etched as Cringe for all those that visit the canyon afterwards.

The Canyon's Magic allows the meme sculptures to imitate their sounds when the canyon is yelled to, as the echo significantly amplifies the cringe.

Visitors of the Canyon are advised to not yell into it, and to quietly enjoy the memes... for many have yelled and many have died...

The Meme Trail

Hacklantis Island is known for its very high density and diversity of memes.

These memes come from all over the island, with every location leaving its mark on the island's culture.

However, analysts have noticed that there is a path that many memes take on their journey through the island.

These analysts have dubbed this route "The Meme Trail".

The Meme Trail begins in the Forbidden Sector, where an anomalous number of memes originate from.

The memes then move westward, going through the Discouraged Sector and Wetwater Lake.

The course changes northward as memes move through Trollcave and Big Dune before passing the The Silly Silly Hilly Silly Hills.

Passing the Tomb of Grass and Giggles, where they meet the memes from past, they go towards Exxtreeme Mountenz before entering Cringe Territory.

Moving through the Cringe Borderlands, Cringe Plateau, and finally Cringe Canyon, they reverse their course.

The memes are last spotted in the Opinion Dominion, then they move back to the Tomb, this time for good.

While these memes may die, the Cringe Canyon stands as a memoriam for memes from all parts of the island across all timelines.
